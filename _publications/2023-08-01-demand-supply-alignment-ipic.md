In hyperconnected supply chain networks, production options are not fixed — they can shift dynamically across distributed manufacturers and facilities. This paper develops a framework for achieving demand-supply alignment in such environments, enabling supply chain operators to match fluctuating demand with available production capacity in near real time. The approach integrates data-driven demand sensing with optimization-based production allocation, demonstrating improved alignment performance over traditional static approaches.
