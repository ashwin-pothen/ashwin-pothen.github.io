This paper presents action research conducted at Georgia Tech during the COVID-19 pandemic on the design, implementation, and continuous improvement of a system for efficient and resilient supply and distribution of personal protective equipment. We propose an end-to-end systemic approach that combines hyperconnectivity and autonomous operations — including smart modular containers, vision sensors, smart dispensers, drones, droids, robots, and smart lockers — inspired by Physical Internet concepts. The framework provides a blueprint for how autonomous technologies can be integrated into critical-product supply chains to improve resilience and responsiveness.
