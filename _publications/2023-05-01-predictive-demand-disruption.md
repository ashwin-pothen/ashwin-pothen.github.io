Demand disruptions — unexpected deviations from forecast demand — can cascade through supply chain networks and cause significant operational and financial impact. This paper introduces a complementary signaling methodology that detects deviations in demand time series early, generating structured warning signals before the disruption propagates downstream. By providing decision-makers with timely, actionable information, the framework enables proactive supply chain repositioning rather than reactive response.
