This paper addresses the challenge of demand forecasting accuracy in hyperconnected logistics networks, where multiple organizations share data. We propose an ensemble approach that leverages both client-side and vendor-side data streams to generate more accurate and robust demand predictions than either source alone. The framework demonstrates that hyperconnected data sharing across organizational boundaries can yield measurable improvements in supply chain planning.
