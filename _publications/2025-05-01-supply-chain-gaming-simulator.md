Traditional supply chain education often relies on simplified, stylized simulations that fail to capture the complexity of real-world operations. This paper presents a data-driven gaming simulator grounded in realistic supply chain data and dynamics. By immersing learners in scenarios that closely mirror operational realities — including demand uncertainty, disruptions, and multi-stakeholder coordination — the simulator enables more effective and engaging supply chain education. The approach bridges the gap between academic training and practitioner-level decision-making.
