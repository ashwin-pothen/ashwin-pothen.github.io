Presented an end-to-end framework for autonomous and hyperconnected distribution of critical products, including findings from a Georgia Tech case study on PPE distribution during the COVID-19 pandemic. The work was published in IFAC-PapersOnLine, vol. 56(2), pp. 7579–7584.
